# Pricing and Plans

Reclaim Protocol offers flexible pricing tiers to match your project's stage. All plans include access to secure, privacy-preserving verification.

## Hacker Tier (Free)

The Hacker tier is for building MVPs, prototypes, and demos.

**Includes:**
- 25 verifications per month
- 1 application
- Access to all community providers

**Best for:** MVPs and demos

## Builder Tier ($99/month)

The Builder tier lets you create custom providers and build unlimited applications.

**Includes:**
- 100 proofs per month
- Access to building your own providers
- Unlimited applications

**Best for:** Building and getting early user feedback

## Startup Tier ($149/month)

The Startup tier includes additional support and AI-powered tools for teams approaching launch.

**Includes:**
- 500 proofs per month
- AI assistance for building providers
- Weekly automated testing
- Dedicated email support

**Best for:** Early stage startups near a public launch

## Enterprise

The Enterprise tier is for high-volume production applications requiring custom support.

**Includes:**
- Volume-based discounted pricing
- Automated and human testing
- On-call engineering support

**Contact us for pricing:** Visit the [payment page](https://dev.reclaimprotocol.org/payment) or reach out to discuss your needs.

## Manage Your Plan

You can view and manage your subscription from the [Reclaim Dev Tool payment page](https://dev.reclaimprotocol.org/payment).
